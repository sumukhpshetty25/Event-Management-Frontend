CEO and Founder

SUMUKH P SHETTY

Sumukh is the Co-founder of Planorama event management company, bringing a unique blend of creativity and strategic planning to the industry. With a background in marketing and hospitality, Sumukh plays a pivotal role in shaping the company's vision and executing high-profile events. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Marketing and a Master's degree in Hospitality Management. With over 9 years of experience in the event management industry, he has successfully overseen numerous high-profile events, from corporate conferences to luxury weddings. His strategic thinking and attention to detail ensure that every event is meticulously planned and flawlessly executed.
